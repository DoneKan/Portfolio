About Me

Hello! I'm Kigozi Kimuli Duncan, a passionate Python developer with a background in Mechatronics Engineering. I hail from Uganda and have had the privilege of working in diverse environments, including Thailand. My journey into the world of technology has been an exciting one, and I continue to expand my knowledge and skills.

Education and Skills

I hold a Bachelors's degree in Mechatronics Engineering, and I've complemented it with various certifications and ongoing ITIL training. My technical expertise includes proficiency in Python, web development (HTML, CSS, JavaScript), and database management (SQL, MySQL, Oracle). Additionally, I am well-versed in operating systems like Mac OS, Windows, and Windows Server.

Professional Experince

Throughout my career, I've had the opportunity to contribute my skills in several IT roles. Presently, I serve as the Head of IT Support at Powell Pay UG LTD, where I manage both physical and online IT support for clients, and actively engage in the coding process to improve management systems. Previously, I was an IT Support Officer at Mukono Hospital C.O.U and D&T Computech Solutions, where I provided technical support, managed hardware and software installations, and worked on network configurations and data analysis.

Projects and Contributions

I'm passionate about leveraging technology to address real-world challenges. In my spare time, I've contributed to the Python community through personal projects on my GitHub repository (https://github.com/DoneKan). I also enjoy graphic design and content creation, which I utilized during my time at D&T Computech Solutions.

Future Goals

Looking ahead, I'm committed to continuous learning and honing my Python skills. I aim to contribute to cutting-edge projects that utilize Python's capabilities to the fullest. Collaborating with like-minded professionals is something I value, as it fosters an environment of growth and innovation.
